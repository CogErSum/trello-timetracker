Privacy Policy

TeamSight Tracker for Trello

1. Data We Collect

TeamSight Tracker collects the following data from Trello:

  • Trello Member ID — used to associate time records with your account
  • Card IDs — used to track time per card
  • Time records — duration, timestamps, and optional comments you enter
  • Time estimates — estimates you set on cards

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the time tracking functionality:

  • Display time tracking history on your cards
  • Show time statistics on your dashboard
  • Generate CSV/Excel exports upon your request

3. Data Storage & Security

Where we store data: All data is stored on secure servers hosted by Render (PostgreSQL database, EU region).

Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest.

Access control: Only the authenticated user (identified by their Trello Member ID) can access their own data. We implement strict access controls at the application level.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer your personal data to any third parties. Your data is:

  • Never sold to advertisers or data brokers
  • Never shared with other Trello users
  • Only accessible through the TeamSight Tracker application
  • Not used for analytics or tracking purposes

5. Data Retention

Your time records and estimates are retained until you delete them or remove the Power-Up from your Trello board. You can delete individual records at any time from the card view.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your stored data
  • Delete individual time records
  • Remove the Power-Up to stop data collection

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at: hello@tmst.io

We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Material changes will be communicated via email to affected users.

9. Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Trello Power-Up Developer Terms of Service
  • Atlassian Marketplace Privacy Policy Requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
